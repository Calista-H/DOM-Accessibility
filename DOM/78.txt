<div class="tsg-rwd-sidebar">
<h3>Processing Times</h3>
<div class="tsg-rwd-sidebar-icon tsg_rwd_hide">
<span class="fa fa-bell fa-4x"></span>
</div>
<div class="tsg-rwd-sidebar-copy tsg_rwd_textbox_full">
<p><b>Routine:</b> 10-13 weeks*<b></b></p>
<p><b>Expedited: </b>7-9 weeks and an extra $60*</p>
<p>*Mailing times are not included in <a adhocenable="false" href="/content/travel/en/passports/how-apply/processing-times.html" title="Processing Times link">processing times</a>. Processing times only include the time your application is at one of our passport agencies or centers. The total time to get your passport includes both processing and mailing times. It may take up to 2 weeks for applications to arrive by mail at a passport agency or center, and up to 2 weeks for you to receive a completed passport in the mail after we print it. <b>Consider the total time it will take to receive your passport when you are booking travel</b>. </p>
<p><b>Urgent Travel:</b> See our <a adhocenable="false" href="/content/travel/en/passports/get-fast.html" title="Get my Passport Fast info">Get my Passport Fast</a> page. </p>
